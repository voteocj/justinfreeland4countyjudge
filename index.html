About Justin

Justin Freeland is a lifelong resident of Ouachita County. He is running as an Independent candidate, and believes the issues we face are not about republican or democrat; they are 100% a Ouachita County issue we all face together.

He and his wife are raising their family here and are committed to keeping our county a safe and thriving place to live and work.

Priorities

Accessible County Government

County government should be responsive and easy to navigate. I will improve communication and streamline permits, records, and services so residents receive clear answers and timely assistance.

Prioritizing Justice

I am committed to listening to your concerns and fighting for solutions that work for everyone.

Collective Action

Real change happens when a community acts together. By listening to residents and encouraging participation, we can build solutions that reflect the will of Ouachita County.

Protecting Essential Services

When vital services like healthcare show signs of strain, we cannot afford to ignore it. I will prioritize collaboration and accountability to help safeguard the services our community depends on.
